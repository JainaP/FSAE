Partnership and Benefits

IIT Motorsports strives to create long lasting partnerships in order to gain knowledge and reach a mutually beneficial arrangement. W e are searching for not only support financially, but also through equipment, parts and expertise. Therefore, we offer an attractive platform for our partners:

  • Access to high potentials
    • As a sponsor you are in direct contact to our members. This is the perfect chance to see your interns or future employees in real professional situations
  • Branding and communication platform
    • An opportunity to not only market your products, but also your business as a future employer for bright minds participating in the FSAE challenge
  • Creating a sustainable Partnership
    • IIT Motorsports is interested in long-lasting relationships. The students of IIT are encouraged to work and act interdisciplinary. Our partnership should be more than money or components as we would like to organize events together and serve as a unique platform for ideas and problem solving solutions.
